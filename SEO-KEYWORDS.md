## Launch checklist — required to actually rank #1 (do these after deploying to uisstore.net)

Code alone cannot reach position 1; these off-page steps are essential:

1. **Google Search Console** — verify uisstore.net, submit `https://uisstore.net/sitemap.xml`, request indexing.
2. **Bing Webmaster Tools** — same (Bing powers many AI answers, including parts of ChatGPT browsing).
3. **Google Business Profile** — create a profile for "UiS Store / Uniweb IT Solutions", Sama Tower address, phone +965 65702446, website uisstore.net, category "Website designer / E-commerce service". This is the single biggest lever for local "ecommerce kuwait" searches and Google Maps.
4. **NAP consistency** — same Name/Address/Phone on the website, Google Business Profile, Instagram bio, LinkedIn, and any Kuwait business directories (Kuwait Yellow Pages, 4SQ, local chambers).
5. **Backlinks** — link uisstore.net from uniwebonline.com, all 6 social profiles, and client sites' footers ("Powered by UiS Store" credit links are very effective).
6. **Reviews** — collect Google reviews from your 60+ clients; rating stars dramatically improve click-through.
7. **Content** — add a blog over time ("How to start an online store in Kuwait", "KNET vs credit card fees") to own the long-tail question keywords; this is also what Gemini/AI assistants cite most.
8. **Arabic version** — a true `/ar` page would unlock section 5 keywords properly (hreflang ar-KW). Currently the site is English with Arabic support messaging.
9. **Monitor** — Search Console → Performance tab; expect brand keywords to rank within days, commercial keywords over 2–6 months as authority builds.
